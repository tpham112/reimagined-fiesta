# Background Check

Processing steps when a law enforcement investigator requests a background check.

## Steps

Step 1. Have the investigator **sign in**.

    !!! note
        Sign-In sheet can be found behind the Administrative Assistant's desk.

Step 2: **Verify their badge**.

Step 3: Take the **release form** — this is **REQUIRED**.

Step 4: Have the investigator **provide their business card** and tape to the release form.

Step 5: Print out **official transcripts on regular paper** and give to the investigator.
