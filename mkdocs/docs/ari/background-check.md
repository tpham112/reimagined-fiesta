# Background Check

Processing steps when a law enforcement investigator requests a background check.

## Steps

1. Have the investigator **sign in**.

    !!! note
        Sign-In sheet can be found the Administrative Assistant's desk.

2. **Verify their badge**.
3. Take the **release form** — this is **REQUIRED**.
4. Have the investigator **write their name and credentials** on the release form.
5. Print out **official transcripts on regular paper** and give to the investigator.
