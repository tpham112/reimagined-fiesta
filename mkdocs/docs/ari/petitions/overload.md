# Overload Petition

## Overview

Students will need an Overload Petition if they want to take more than

- Spring/Fall
    - 18 units
- Summer
    - 9 units
- Spring Intersession
    - 6 units

---

## Processing Steps

1. Access the Student Record
    - Navigate to **Colleague → STPE**.
    - Enter the **Student ID** and press **Enter**.
2. Add a New Petition
    - Enter `A` and click **Ok**.
    - Fill in the following fields

    | **Field** | **Details** |
    |-----------|-------------|
    | Term | Enter the desired term |
    | Course | Enter the **Course Subject and Number**; double-check to confirm the **Start Date is correct** and **Status is A (Active)**; double-click on the desired course. |
    | Petition Status | Enter `APPR` |
    | Petition Reason | Enter `APPR` |
    | Overload Petition Status | Enter `APPR` |

    ![Fill in the following fields](../../images/overload1.png)

3. Save Changes
    - Click **Save All**.
