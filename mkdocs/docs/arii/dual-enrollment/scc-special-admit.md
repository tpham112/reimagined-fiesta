# SCC Special Admit Forms

A&R staff may process Santiago Canyon College (SCC) Special Admit forms received in our office.

## Procedure

1. **Accept and review the form** received in our office.
    - Do **not** redirect students to SCC for processing.
    - Verify all required sections are complete: student, parent/guardian, and high school approvals.

2. **Follow registration rules** — ensure all eligibility requirements are met and courses align with SCC's Special Admit guidelines.

3. **Students below 9th grade:** SCC requires the **Dean's signature**. Confirm it is present before processing.

!!! info "Reciprocal Processing"
    SCC also processes Santa Ana College (SAC) Special Admit forms submitted at their office.
