# Subpoenas

!!! warning "Needs to Be Reviewed for Updates"
    This procedure may need to be reviewed for any policy updates.

---

## 1. Notice to Student

- If a **Release of Authorization** form is attached to the subpoena, **no mailed notice is required**.
- If no release is attached:
    1. Prepare the **Letter and Notice to Consumer or Employee**.
    2. Place the notice in an envelope and mail to the student using:
        - **Certified Mail**
        - **Return Receipt Requested**
    3. Deliver the prepared envelope and certified mail forms to **Bee in the Mail Room**.
    4. Upon receipt:
        - Attach the **returned receipt** to the filed subpoena.
        - If the mail is returned, attach the returned mail to the filed subpoena as well.
- **Wait 7 calendar days** for a response.
- If no response is received after 7 days, begin processing the subpoena.

---

## 2. Review and Distribution

1. Review the subpoena to determine **what records are being requested**.
2. Forward the subpoena to all applicable departments to gather the required information.
3. Compile all received documentation.
4. Send completed records and documents to **Risk Management**.

---

## 3. Records Collection

### A. Application Records
- **LaserFiche** — Print the student's application.
- **OpenCCCApply Report Center** — Print the student's application.

### B. Transcripts (TRAN)
1. Enter the **Student ID**.
2. Save.
3. P → FormFusion
4. Use the printing code.
5. Save and update.

### C. Other Student Records (If Requested)
Copy all relevant correspondence and student records including instructor rosters, grades, and programs. Ensure all documents are in order and legible. **Only include the requested student's records.** Redact or white out other students' information as needed.

### D. Full Grades and Rosters (If Required)
1. **STAC** — Print course list.
2. **M: Drive → Instructor Grades:**
    - Search by semester.
    - Search by instructor and section.
    - Locate rosters for each registered class.
3. Redact or black out all other students' information.
4. Convert all documents to PDF and ensure redactions are applied correctly.

---

Email the department(s) with the subpoena request and student name/ID. Request that documentation be sent via **interoffice mail** if applicable.

---

## 5. Subpoena Fees Form

- Track all time spent processing the subpoena for billing purposes.
- Copy the tracked time from Excel and paste it as an image into a letterhead document.
- Save the file as **Subpoena Fees**.
- Convert the document to PDF by right-clicking on the file.

---

## 6. Custodian of Records Form

- Complete the **Custodian of Records** form provided by HR.
- Attach the completed form to the subpoena package.

---

## 7. Assembly, Sending, and Filing

### Document Order

1. Custodian of Records Form
2. Subpoena Fees Form
3. Application
4. Transcripts
5. Additional subpoena-requested documents

### Distribution and Filing

- Make copies of all documents.
- Email the completed subpoena package to **Annina Brown**.
- File physical copies in the **first drawer located by Mark's office**.
- Include the **notification letter to the student** in the file.
    - If the letter is returned, file it with the subpoena documentation.

---

## 8. Special Notes and Exceptions

!!! warning "Public Defender Subpoenas"
    Do **not** pay the $15 fee.

!!! info "Workers' Compensation Subpoenas"
    - Include **application and transcripts only**.
    - Send documents to the **District**.
    - No fee letter is required.
    - Print official transcripts from **TRAN**.
