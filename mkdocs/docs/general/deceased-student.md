# Deceased Student Notification Process

## Goals

- To provide a sensitive and caring response to family members.
- To permanently close the student's academic records.
- To follow a uniform procedure that properly informs all departments/staff listed below.

---

## Steps

### 1. Initial Notification
When a staff or faculty member is notified of a student's death, they will notify the **Registrar/Associate Dean of Admissions**.

### 2. Obtain Documentation
Information about the student is obtained from family, faculty, counselor, or other person. Obtain **proof of death** (documentation can be a newspaper obituary or article). The sheet and documentation will be scanned and placed in the student's file.

### 3. Registrar/Associate Dean Notifications
The Registrar/Associate Dean within Admissions will contact the following staff via email, phone call, or personal visit:

| Contact | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| College President and Executive Secretary | Mails a letter of condolence |
| Vice President of Instruction and Student Services | For information only |
| Faculty | Notification if student is active in classes |
| Financial Aid | Check if the student was receiving aid |
| Special Programs (EOPS, DSPS) | For coordination |
| Graduation Office | Check if a petition is on file |
| Cashier's Office | Check if the student has outstanding fees |
| Associate Dean of Student Development | For information only |
| Communications Specialist, Public Affairs | If media becomes involved |
| Psychological Services | If counseling and support may be needed |
| Scholarship Office | Check if student is receiving a monetary award |
| Human Resources or Job Placement | In case student was employed within the district |

### 4. Colleague Updates (Datatel)

Admissions will perform the following in Colleague/Datatel:

- **NAE:** Type `DECEASED` in the address line.
- **NAE:** Add the mail code `DE` to eliminate the possibility of mailing to this person.
- **NAE:** Remove the email address.
- **BIO:** Change the **Directory field** to `N` (no) to exclude from FERPA releases.
- **BIO:** Enter `Deceased` in the status field.
- **PERC:** Place a **deceased hold** on the student's record.
- **DEC:** Enter deceased student data.
- Check for hard copies of transcripts. Request SAC to attach a note stating **DECEASED** on the hard copy.

---

## Important Notes

!!! danger
    Do **not** release any information on a deceased student without a **subpoena**.

!!! info "Family Transcript Requests"
    Sometimes family members request the student's transcript. They must show a copy of the **death certificate** and **ID** to identify their relationship to the student.
