# Early Decision (ARII)

## Material Checklist

Before the event, have these ready:

- Paper applications
- Paper AB 540 forms
- Paper Reclass forms

---

## Troubleshooting Early Decision Students

### Students Arriving Without a Complete Application

**No SAC Application on File:**

1. Student must create a CCC ID on CCCApply.
2. Provide a paper application.
3. Admissions staff will manually enter the application.
4. Student can register within an hour once processed.

**Student Claims They Applied but No Record Is Found:**

- **Option 1:** Ask student to log into CCCApply and provide their confirmation number and CCCApply ID → load a paper application.
- **Option 2:** If student cannot log in or retrieve details → provide a paper application and follow "No SAC Application on File" steps.

**Student Has a Spring Application but No Summer/Fall Application:**

- Provide a paper application (they should already have a CCC ID).
- Load the paper application.

---

### Updating Student Residency

**AB 540 Students:**

- Students should submit the AB 540 Dynamic Form.
    - If no SSO access: accept a paper AB 540 form.
- Admissions staff manually updates residency status.

**Non-Resident Students:**

- **Option 1 (documentation):**
    - **ID:** California ID or school ID (Aeries screenshot with student photo).
    - **Transcripts:** HS transcript showing courses at a CA high school for the past 2 years.
    - **Citizenship:** HS Demographic Report showing U.S. citizenship, or HS transcript showing U.S. as birthplace.
- **Option 2 (no documentation available):**
    - Student must submit a Reclass Form (via Dynamic Form or paper).

### Registering for Classes

- Students register through **Self-Service**.
- **No paper Add Cards.**

### Students w/ 1PF Holds

**High School Students**

- We can accept student transcript and Aeries ID
    - Make note in PERC that we saw the student at Early Decision and verified that they are a real person and we saw their transcripts and ID.
    - Do **not** need to file.

**Adult Education Students**

- We can accept a California ID through the [Fraud Verification Form](https://nextgensso.com/sp/startSSO.ping?PartnerIdpId=http://adfs.rsccd.edu/adfs/services/trust&SpSessionAuthnAdapterId=rsccdSP&TargetResource=https%3a%2f%2fdynamicforms.ngwebsolutions.com%2fSubmit%2fStart%2ffcef4290-2969-47bc-b935-3bf528655d66).

### ULINK / Puente

Admissions enters CORM flags:

**COAF:**

1. Enter Student ID.
2. Go to an empty line.
3. Enter **`1PUEN`** (Puente) or **`1ULIN`** (ULINK) → press Enter.
4. Enter the highlighted fields.
5. Detail into Comments and leave a note that this was authorized by the specialist from Puente/U-Link/U2 Scholars.
5. **Save All.**
